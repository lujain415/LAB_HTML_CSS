What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to simulate human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making. Today, AI is used in many fields like medicine, education, and marketing.

Its Impact on Our Lives

AI has entered our daily lives in many ways, such as voice assistants (Siri, Alexa), recommendation systems on Netflix and YouTube, and even self-driving cars.

The Future of AI

The future of AI holds exciting advancements, but also challenges like privacy, data security, and ethical concerns that must be addressed.
